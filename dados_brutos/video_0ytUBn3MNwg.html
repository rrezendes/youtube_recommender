INSAID Immersion series brings Data Science & AI Leaders across the world to share relevant developments and insights with aspiring Data Scientists.

In this video, Lavi Nigam, Data Scientist at Gartner talks about:

- What is Data Science Modelling
- How do we make these models useful in the production
- Integrating models into Products
- Tools and Techniques that are required
- Best practices and addressing scalability concerns

Lavi has been practicing Data Science and AI at work for close to 6 years now. He has been instrumental in helping a lot of companies solve complex data science problems and build powerful solutions around them. He is an active trainer and is very popular in the student community as well.

DATA SCIENTIST DEGREE // One of the most common questions people ask me is “what data scientist degree do I need?” Or, they might say, I have a ____ degree. Can I be a data analyst or data scientist?

This week I’m going to break the answer down in more detail and talk about the data scientist degree you need.

There are great data analyst degree programs and data science degree programs out there. If you have the chance to go through one of them, they’ll be helpful in preparing you for your career.

However, if you’re already in the workforce or soon going to be graduating, you might be wondering if you need to continue your education in order to get into analytics.

If you have a technical background of any sort, chances are strong that you have the data science degree you need.

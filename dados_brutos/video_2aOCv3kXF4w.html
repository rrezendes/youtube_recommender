The Python Show #5, late-night edition 🌙

Links:
- BIG DIVE, courses for data scientists: https://www.bigdive.eu
- Volunteer online for Europython 2020: https://ep2020.europython.eu/registra...

🎥 Agenda:

- Astra Stefania Delprete (Turin): "Open source communities and the BIG DIVE trainings"
https://twitter.com/astrastefania

Stefania is a data scientist and a well-known Python activist from Italy. She will tell us about one of the projects she is involved in - the BigDive, an intense training program for data scientists, backed by the non-profit innovation consortium TOP-IX.

- Marc-Andre Lemburg (Düsseldorf): "EuroPython Backstage: Moving from in-presence to online"
https://twitter.com/malemburg

Europython was forced to move online, which created a big debate all over Europe. "But I go to meet my friends", "How about the community spirit", "Why would I pay X for an online event" - perhaps all of us had at least one of those questions in mind. This show is our chance to get answers from the primary source - directly from the main organizer of Europython, who'll be our guest.

- Miroslav Šedivý (Karlsruhe): "Python, Let's Go Home. Quickly."
https://twitter.com/eumiro

When your daily commute, shopping tour, or visit to family or friends offers many possible routes and real-time online navigation services are of no use for your individual mean of transport, you can still learn to find the optimal route.

- Boosted Boris 🥁
Check out the blog of our crazy drummer: https://medium.com/@boriszn

At the end of the show, everyone is invited to join a public skype call and talk about everything off-record.

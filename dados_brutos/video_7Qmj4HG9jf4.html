INSAID Immersion series brings Data Science & AI Leaders across the world to share relevant developments and insights with aspiring Data Scientists.

In this video, Akshay Sehgal, General Manager, Data Science at Reliance Industries talks about:

- His transition into Data Science.
- How is Reliance is using Data Science to dominate the Indian market?
- How companies in India have evolved in Data Science?
- How to build a successful career in Data Science?

Before Reliance Industries, Akshay was employed at Mu Sigma and had run his own startup as well. He has helped drive important business decisions based on data for small and large businesses.

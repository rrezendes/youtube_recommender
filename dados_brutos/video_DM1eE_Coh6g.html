Mistake 1: Forgetting to add github and kaggle links
Mistake 2: Resume is too generic
Mistake 3: Focus on algorithms not outcomes
Mistake 4: Descriptions are too vague
Mistake 5: Telling rather than showing
Mistake 6: No personality in resume
Mistake 7: Grammatical errors
Mistake 8: No projects

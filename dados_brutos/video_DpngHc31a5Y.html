People, process, & technology: these universal levers of organizations have defined every business that’s ever been built. In this session, we’ll discuss how Google Cloud’s approach to data analytics technology is empowering people and opening up new opportunities through data-driven business processes. We'll share how our customers are leveraging GCP’s serverless solutions to redefine data’s relationship with business, examining enterprise data warehousing, streaming analytics, managed Spark and Hadoop, modern BI, planet-scale data lakes, AI, and more.

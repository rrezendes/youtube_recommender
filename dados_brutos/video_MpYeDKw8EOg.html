In this episode, Sanyam Bhutani interviews Kaggle legend, Double Kaggle Grand Master and Former number one in Kaggle Competitions: Gilberto Titericz, aka Giba on Kaggle.

They talk all about Giba's journey into the field of data science broadly speaking, and also on Kaggle. Giba shares many amazing advises about Kaggle and also connect the dots for us of how those connect to the real world of data science.

There are many advices about Kaggle, about data science and how to get your first break into data science.

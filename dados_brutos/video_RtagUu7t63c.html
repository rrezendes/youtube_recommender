Publicado em 3 de abr. de 2019

Deep Learning is a hot topic and relevant to the future of GIS. In this session, we will cover the newest tools in ArcGIS Pro and ArcGIS Enterprise for integrating deep learning models into ArcGIS. You will learn how you can use ArcGIS to leverage your existing GIS data and imagery to generate training data. You will learn how to integrate models built with common frameworks such as TensorFlow, CNTK, and PyTorch. We will also cover how custom frameworks and models can be integrated into ArcGIS.

Yauhen Babakhin holds a Master’s Degree in Applied Data Analysis and has over 4 years of working experience in Data Science. He worked in Banking, Gaming and eCommerce domains specializing in NLP problems such as Text Classification, Sequence Labeling, Language Modeling, etc. Now, he starts working at H2O.ai company as a Data Scientist. Yauhen is the first Kaggle competitions Grandmaster in Belarus. He has gold medals in both classic Machine Learning and Deep Learning competitions. At the moment, Yauhen is developing an online course on how to win Kaggle competitions for Datacamp.

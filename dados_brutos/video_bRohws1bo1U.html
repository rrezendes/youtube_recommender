In this video, I talk about 8 reasons that you shouldn't become a data scientist. Below are eight reasons which explain why data science/machine learning isn't the right fit for you.

1) You are thinking about data science because of the hype & money
2) You have a fear of failure
3) You are uncomfortable with statistics
4) You don't like coding
5) You hate doing a google search to solve machine learning problems
6) You hate cleaning data
7) You don't see the value of simple tasks in business
8) You are reluctant in imbibing new technology

This MIT School of Architecture and Planning (MIT SA+P) Data Science in Real Estate online short course introduces you to the application of machine learning in the built environment, while teaching you to use data science methods to identify and interpret patterns to analyze property risks and opportunities.

Over six weeks, you’ll explore what it means to be a good data scientist, from both a technical and ethical perspective, as you gain the practical data science skills to make informed, data-driven investment decisions.

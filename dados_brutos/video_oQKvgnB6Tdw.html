Both Python and R will help you analyze data to see valuable business insights. If you’re just getting started or debating learning a new programming language though, it’s helpful to focus on building expertise in 1 language. Let’s look at the benefits of R vs Python for Data Science. We’ll look at employability, how each language is used, and the difficulty of learning it.

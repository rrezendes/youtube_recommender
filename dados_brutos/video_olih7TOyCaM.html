Data science is related to data mining, deep learning and big data. Data scientists are analytical data expert who has the technical skills to solve complex problems. This video will give you information on what data science is exactly, how to be a data scientist in India.

Data Science is a science or study of data, and involves creating algorithms. Data scientists choose and build appropriate algorithms. Due to its multi-disciplinary nature, Data Science requires you to have a broad set of skills.

One requires the knowledge of Mathematics, Statistics, Computer Science and Coding. One can either pursue the Commerce or Science stream to build a career in data science. Knowledge about the concepts of Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning.

There are many good online learning platforms that offer certifications. Skill required:
Languages like Java, Perl, C/C++, Python
Softwares like SAS, R, Hadoop and Tableau

Top undergraduate institutes to pursue fields related to data science are:
1.Indian statistical institute
2.Delhi university
3. IIT Kanpur
4.IIT Bombay
5.Indian Institution of science education and research

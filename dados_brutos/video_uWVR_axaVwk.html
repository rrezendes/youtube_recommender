Abhishek Thakur is the chief data scientist at https://boost.ai building state-of-the-art chatbots primarily for banking and insurance industries. His passion lies in solving difficult world problems through data science. He is the co-organizer of the Berlin Machine Learning Meetup and not long ago was ranked no. 3 worldwide on the Data Science Platform Kaggle. He is a Competitions and Discussions Grandmaster. Abhishek did his Bachelors in Electronics Engineering from India and moved to Germany for pursuing MSc from University of Bonn, Germany with a focus on image processing and computer vision. He dropped out of PhD in 2015 and since then has been working in industries.
